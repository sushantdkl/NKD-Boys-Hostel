Our Story

The Hostel That Became Home

For over 4 years, NKD Boys Hostel has been the most beloved student residence in Kathmandu. We started with one simple belief: students deserve more than just a bed — they deserve a thriving environment.

Our experienced wardens treat residents like family. Our chef has been cooking the same legendary dal-bhat for years (residents refuse to let him change the recipe 😄). From multiple entrances to spacious parking, everything is designed for your comfort.
